Maybe his second week will go better.
<pad> A play on the word " week "  ---  figur ative ly ,  a second week in  a relationship is  a better outcome  if the person is having  a good week or having  a bad week . Here , however , the second week is literal , and so the phrase is  figur ative ly : the first week went pretty bad ( liter ally ) and the person is hopeful that the second week will be better ( figur ative ly , they ' ll get along better ). </s>
